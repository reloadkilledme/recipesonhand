About Us

 I am Leo Reid, founder of Recipes on Hand. I created this site and app with my girlfriend Kat. We love to cook and somethings we cannot figure out what to make but we know we have a ton of ingredients at home. Some days it would be easier to just select “Find Recipe” and give us options. After each time we grocery shop we would write down what we have on the refrigerator. But we would always forget the smaller items, so this would help us remember all our extra items.
