User Guide

  1. Computer Use:
    1. Home Page
      1. This will serve as a splash page. This will have navigation (navigation will be on each page) and display our purpose. In the navigation you will have options to selection for Home Input Ingredients (inside ingredients page you will have a “Find Recipes” button), Food Safety and User Guide. It will allow you to log in if you have created an account, creating account saves your information.
      1. Input Ingredients
        1. This is where you will input your food that you have in your house. Once you have entered in all your food you can select “Find Recipes” and it will display the potential recipes you can make.
        1. Food Safety
          1. This is where you will find information about food safety and cook temperatures. You will also have a link to the FDA website for additional food safety guidelines.
          1. User Guide
            1. You will find the user guide here along with frequently asked questions
